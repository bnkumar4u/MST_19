Malaviya Sports Tournament'19

About us

Malaviya Sports Tournament (MST) started in 2007. Having become possibly the biggest sports tournament in Rajasthan, it only had about 300 participants from 12 different institutions in its inaugural edition. As the years have gone by, the number of participating institutions and thereby students has increased drastically. Scheduled to take place from the 21st - 23rd of February, participants of MST '19 will be showing their mettle in a plethora of sports events including cricket, basketball, kabbadi, volleyball, weightlifting, chess, badminton, table tennis, and powerlifting. As participation grows, new events may also be announced in the future! Of course, MST '19 doesn't cater merely to the sportspersons or fans out there - there will be a lot more going on, exciting and fun events, cultural activities galore. Although a lot of anticipation has been building for this edition of MST, the tournament is promised to live up to and even beyond all expectations. MST '19 will be the most entertaining, most competitive, most electrifying sports tournament you have ever witnessed.
